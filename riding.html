Riding

With miles of scenic trails and open fields, Hilltop Ranch is the perfect place for both beginners and experienced riders. As per your experience level, take a guided walk, trot, canter, or gallop through our trails on one of our friendly and safe horses. You'll get to see varying landscapes and wildlife as we wind through trails, over creeks and through the woods. Adults (18+) only.

Looking to bring kids? We offer supervised pony rides for kids 7 and under and riding lessons for children ages 7-18.
